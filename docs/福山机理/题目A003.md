# 题目A003



## 反应

羧酸与亚硫酰氯的反应。

![377](assets/A003/file-20260611154000451.svg){width="600"}



## 机理一

![656](assets/A003/file-20260611154109768.svg){width="700"}

步骤A: Attack of a carboxylic acid to $\ce{SOCl2}$ forms a mixed anhydride. 羧酸进攻 $\ce{SOCl2}$ 形成混合酸酐。

步骤B: Addition of chloride ion to the carbonyl group to form a tetrahedral intermediate. 氯离子进攻羰基碳，形成四面体中间体。

## 机理二

![628](assets/A003/file-20260611154138590.svg){width="700"}

步骤C: Formation of an acylium ion. 酰基正离子的形成

步骤D: Addition of chloride ion to the acylium ion. 氯离子进攻酰基正离子。
